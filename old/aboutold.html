About Us

We are a group of teenagers based in London aspiring to make a website to deliver all of the latest tech news to you, wether it be about the newest iOS release or a new Samsung phone, we are on it. But what is our story? Well, we started construction on the 24th Jan 2025 and this is an ongoing project to expand our knowledge of coding and website design. Our logo was designed by me and Rohan inside our school library one day after making the idea, the sole purpose of this website is to get the latest tech news as fast as possible, no beating around the bush, no, just sole tech news, no interferences. Also, to set the record, we didnt use any website builders, just raw html, css and javascript. Click the "SOCIAL MEDIA" link inside the header at the top to view our youtube channel, be sure to subscribe!
